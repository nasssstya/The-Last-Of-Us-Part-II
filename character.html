Joel Miller

On the surface, Joel can seem like any other
rough and stoic video game protagonist, but
there's a lot more to this experienced survivor
than initially meets the eye. After losing his
daughter Sarah in the outbreak, Joel would
become a coldhearted survivor whose sense
of morality had clearly been slipping away
after the world had gone to hell.
