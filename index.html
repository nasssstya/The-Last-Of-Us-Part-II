Answers to questions about «The Last Of Us Part II»

What is the release date for The Last of Us: Part II?

The Last of Us Part II was officially released on June 19, 2020 for PlayStation 4.

Does this game have anything to do with the HBO series?

The HBO series is directly inspired by the original game and its sequel. It follows the same storyline but includes new details and expanded character arcs.

Does this game include the multiplayer mode "Factions"?

No, the Factions multiplayer mode is not part of The Last of Us Part II. However, Naughty Dog has announced a standalone multiplayer project inspired by it.

Are there any bonuses for pre-ordering a PS5 game?

Yes, players who pre-order the PS5 version may receive in-game rewards such as exclusive skins, weapon upgrades, or early unlockable items.

Why did you decide to release a remake of this game?

The remake was developed to take advantage of the PlayStation 5’s advanced capabilities — providing enhanced graphics, faster loading times, and improved gameplay fidelity.

Does this game include a separate downloadable chapter?

Yes, the remastered edition includes the “Lost Levels” bonus mode and behind-the-scenes commentary from the development team.

What has improved in this version of The Last of Us: Part II?

The PS5 version features higher resolution textures, enhanced lighting, faster loading, DualSense adaptive trigger support, and an improved frame rate up to 60 FPS.

What languages are supported in the game?

The game supports English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Japanese, Korean, and more — with both full voice acting and subtitles.

Will The Last of Us: Part II be released on PC?

As of now, Naughty Dog has not officially confirmed a PC version, but given that Part I was released on PC, a port is possible in the future.

Will the "Realism" difficulty level be available?

Yes, the Realism mode is available in the Remastered edition — it offers minimal HUD, no listening mode, and tougher, more aware enemies for a true survival experience.
